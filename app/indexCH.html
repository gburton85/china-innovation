Get ahead on your career journey.

Congratulations on choosing to study at a university in the UK!

You are about to start an amazing journey that will provide you with new knowledge, opportunities and experiences, as well the chance to make new friends and explore a different part of the world.

Making the most of your time in the UK is an important part of your experience and one of the best ways to do this is to get ahead, and get started on your career journey.

It’s never too early to start your career journey!

Not only do Chinese graduates talk about the importance of planning your career journey, but many job vacancies, internships and work experience opportunities are advertised between September and December, so you may need to start applying as soon as you arrive in the UK.

Getting started

Use this course to create your own career plan that you can download at the end of the course and use to develop your skills and employability whilst you are in the UK, and beyond. Your career plan will help make you more employable and help you get ahead in your chosen career. Plus it’s a great way to understand more about yourself and your goals.
