3. Research Your Options

Understanding more about yourself will help you to start your career journey AND make you more employable.

Use this section to find out more about yourself, your skills and abilities, personality and strengths.

Understanding yourself is the first step in developing your career journey and your career plan. The great news about developing your career plan is that there are no right or wrong answers, as it’s all about you and what you would like to achieve, no matter what your goals are.
